Kara Sun

Welcome to my world, where every day is a journey to discover yourself and understand the beauty of everyday life. This is Kara Sun - I am a seeker of myself, my own essence, a therapist, masseuse and Yoga instructor. In my work and life, I constantly strive for harmony and awareness, celebrating every moment with passion and commitment. My professional experience and passion for working with people allow me to share knowledge and support others in their search for inner harmony and a healthy life. Empathy, a positive attitude to life and a deep interest in people and the world around us are extremely important to me. In my practice, both as a therapist and yoga instructor, I emphasize a deep understanding of life "here and now". I realize that it is not the goal itself but the path to it that is of greatest value. Therefore, I invite you to join on a journey where the only thing that matters is the path leading to inner balance, peace and a deep understanding of life. I am happy to share my passion for life with anyone who is willing to participate in my classes and therapy. I will teach you how to work with the body, which, combined with mental concentration and appropriate breathing, will allow you to fully feel yourself and be here and now.
